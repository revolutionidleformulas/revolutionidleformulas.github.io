Zodiacs

General

Most zodiac variables rely on zodiacs score, which is the culmination of zodiacs level, rarity and quality. Score is calculated using the following formula:

where \( L \) is level, \( Q \) is quality, \( R \) is rarity and \( R_+ \) is the number after rarity (or \( 0 \) for the lack of it).
\[ \text{Zodiac Score} = (9 + L^2) * Q * 1.125^{R + R_+} \times \begin{cases} R \leq 8 & : \ 1 \\ 8 < R & : \ 2 \end{cases} \times \begin{cases} L < 100 & : \ 1 \\ L \geq 100 & : \ (\frac{L - 90}{10})^{2.5} \end{cases} \]

For \( R \), "garbage" means \( 0 \), "common" - \( 1 \), etc., up to immortal (\( 9 \)). Immortal+X are fairly straightforward: \( R_+ = X \).
